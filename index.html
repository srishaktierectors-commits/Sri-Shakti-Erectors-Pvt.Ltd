What We Do

Core Capabilities

Comprehensive mechanical erection services for Oil & Gas, Refinery, and Thermal Power sectors. Our team of skilled engineers, supervisors, and technicians including MW fitters, Piping fitters, IBR welders, Piping welders, and Riggers ensures precision execution, safe work practices, and timely project completion.

Steam Turbine Erection

Complete erection of steam turbine generators including foundation work, alignment, and commissioning support.

Condenser Installation

Expert installation of air-cooled and water-cooled condensers with precision engineering and quality assurance.

Auxiliaries Erection

Installation of all auxiliary equipment including pumps, fans, heat exchangers, and associated systems.

Power Plant Piping

Complete piping works including HP/LP piping, steam lines, water systems, and all interconnecting pipework.

Commissioning Support

Comprehensive commissioning assistance ensuring smooth startup and optimal performance of all systems.

Shutdown Maintenance

Planned shutdown services, preventive maintenance, and emergency repairs to maximize plant availability.
