Our Services

Comprehensive mechanical erection solutions for Oil & Gas, Refinery, and Thermal Power sectors. Specializing in piping works, rotating equipment installation, alignment, testing, and commissioning.

Core Service

Steam Turbine Generator Erection

Complete turnkey erection of steam turbine generators from foundation preparation to final commissioning. Our experienced teams handle all aspects of turbine installation with precision and adherence to OEM specifications.

Scope of Work:

  • Foundation grouting and preparation
  • Turbine base frame installation and alignment
  • HP, IP, and LP turbine assembly and erection
  • Generator stator and rotor installation
  • Coupling alignment (turbine-generator)
  • Steam chest and valve installation
  • Bearing housing and lubrication system setup
  • Pre-commissioning checks and support

Capabilities:

  • Turbines up to 660 MW capacity
  • All major OEMs (Siemens, BHEL, GE, etc.)
  • Precision alignment using laser technology
  • Experienced supervisors and skilled workforce

Condenser Installation

Specialized Service

Air-Cooled & Water-Cooled Condenser Erection

Expert installation of both air-cooled and water-cooled condensers with complete auxiliary systems. We ensure proper alignment, leak-free joints, and optimal performance.

Air-Cooled Condensers:

  • ACC structure erection and alignment
  • Finned tube bundle installation
  • Fan and motor assembly
  • Steam duct and piping connections
  • Condensate collection system

Water-Cooled Condensers:

  • Condenser shell positioning and alignment
  • Tube bundle installation and expansion
  • Water box assembly
  • Circulating water piping
  • Vacuum system installation
  • Hydro testing and leak detection
Complete Package

Auxiliaries Erection

Installation of all auxiliary equipment and systems essential for power plant operation, including pumps, fans, heat exchangers, and associated mechanical equipment.

Equipment Covered:

  • Boiler feed pumps and motors
  • Condensate extraction pumps
  • Circulating water pumps
  • Cooling tower pumps and fans
  • ID, FD, and PA fans
  • Air preheaters
  • Economizers
  • Feed water heaters (HP & LP)
  • Deaerators
  • Coal handling equipment
  • Ash handling systems

Our Approach:

Systematic installation following OEM guidelines, proper foundation preparation, precision alignment, and thorough testing before handover.

Critical Infrastructure

Power Plant Piping Works

Complete piping installation services covering all pressure ranges and fluid systems in thermal power plants, executed to ASME/ANSI standards with full documentation.

Piping Systems:

  • Main steam piping (HP)
  • Hot reheat and cold reheat piping
  • Feed water piping
  • Condensate piping
  • Extraction steam piping
  • Circulating water piping
  • Cooling water systems
  • Drain and vent piping
  • Lubrication oil piping
  • Chemical dosing systems

Services Include:

  • Pipe fabrication and fitting
  • Welding (TIG, MIG, SMAW)
  • Pipe supports and hangers installation
  • Hydro testing and flushing
  • NDT and quality documentation
