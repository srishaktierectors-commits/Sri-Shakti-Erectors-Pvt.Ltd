About Sri Shakti Erectors

A professionally managed organization executing construction projects across Oil & Gas, Refinery, and Thermal Power sectors with precision, safety, and unwavering commitment to excellence.

Our Story

Powering India's Growth

We are a professionally managed organization with extensive experience in executing construction projects in the Oil & Gas, Refinery, and Thermal Power sectors. Our company has successfully contributed to multiple engineering and construction projects, delivering reliable and high-quality services in compliance with industry standards and client requirements.

We specialize in piping works and possess strong expertise in the installation, alignment, testing, and commissioning of rotating equipment, including turbines, pumps, blowers, and ID/FD fans. Our team of skilled engineers, supervisors, and skilled technicians like MW fitters, Piping fitters, IBR welders, Piping welders, and Riggers ensures precision execution, safe work practices, and timely project completion.

With a focus on technical excellence, safety, and customer satisfaction, we are committed to supporting our clients through efficient project execution and dependable after-completion support.
